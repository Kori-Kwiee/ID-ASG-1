About Alien Stage

The Alien Stage is a televised singing competition hosted by the Segyein, where their Human Pets perform musical numbers for a panel of alien judges and a vast extraterrestial audience.

The stakes are absolute:
losers are executed on the spot, while winners are granted temporary reprieve and the opportunity to advance to the next round. The ultimate prize other than survival is unclear, but it's speculated that it could be a form of ambiguous "freedom" or elevated status within the segyein-controlled society. For the segyein, the Alien Stage appears to serve multiple purposes. It is a form of public entertainment, which appears to sate the segyein's morbid fascination with human suffering, emotions and creativity. It may also serve as a way to reinforce segyein dominance, to discourage rebellion, and perhaps a perverse form of population control. For humans, it could serve as a twisted outlet for human ambition.
